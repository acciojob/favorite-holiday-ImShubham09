Maldives


Maldives is a beautiful place best place for vist.it's full of beautiful lakes and ocean

Top 5 attractions

  1. Fushimi Inari Shrine: Famous for its thousands of vibrant torii gates.
  2. Arashiyama Bamboo Grove: A magical walk through towering bamboo stalks.
  3. Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion): A stunning golden temple reflected on a serene pond.
  4. Gion District: Explore the historic geisha district with its charming wooden machiya houses.
  5. Philosopher's Path: A tranquil walk along a canal lined with cherry blossoms in spring.

Must-try foods

  • Kaiseki: A traditional multi-course meal emphasizing seasonal ingredients.
  • Yudofu: A simple and comforting dish of tofu simmered in a light broth.
  • Matcha Desserts: Try green tea-flavored sweets, from cakes to ice creams.
  • Sushi: Kyoto-style sushi, known as oshi-zushi, is pressed sushi with unique toppings.
  • Soba Noodles: Delicious buckwheat noodles served hot or cold.
